Data Format Guidelines

For best results, ensure your sales data includes:

  • Date/Time columns: Order date, sale date, created date
  • Amount columns: Sale amount, price, total, revenue
  • Product columns: Product name, item, category
  • Customer columns: Customer name, client, buyer
